Best Workplaces™ Journey

 

 Organisations that want to be recognised on a Best Workplace™ list, start by getting Great Place to Work – Certified™. Through the certification process, we capture valuable employee feedback using our research-driven Trust Index© survey and details about the programmes and practises that make your workplace unique.

 

 Our 4 Step Best Workplaces™ Process:

 

1. Survey Employees

Select a 2-week period to launch our Trust Index© employee survey. 

2. Culture Audit© 

Provide details about your company’s culture, programmes and practices.

  

3. Get Certified™

If your Trust Index© results are 65% or higher, you'll be Great Place to Work-Certified™.

4. Earn Recognition

Our annual Best Workplaces™ lists are then selected from the pool of Certified™ organisations with 20+ employees.

Certification: Your First Recognition Milestone!

An organisation with 10+ employees will be Certified™ when their overall employee survey results are 65% or higher.

Certified™ organisations receive:

  • Great Place to Work® Certification badge, valid for 12 months
  • Certification Profile and a PR toolkit to help you promote your great workplace to the world
  • Automatic entry into our Best Workplaces™ lists

Earn Recognition On Our Best Workplaces™ Lists

 

 

Certified organisations with 20+ employees will automatically be considered for placement on our Best Workplaces™ lists. We partner with leading media publishers, such as The Guardian and The Sunday Telegraph, to help your employer brand gain top exposure.
